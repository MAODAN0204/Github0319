Machine light does not turn on.

Please press the button again for 5 seconds and restart the machine to see if the light is not on or if it is just in the off state.

If the light still does not turn on after 5 seconds, please contact the factory service personnel.

Machine blinking red light.

The flashing red light indicates that the system has detected the abnormality and carried out corresponding protection measures, when the phenomenon to be protected disappears, the red light will not flash.

If you have any questions, please contact the original customer service personnel.

All five lights on the machine don't turn on.

Please refer to Question 1

No information on APP homepage.

Please add a new device on the second page of the screen and tap the device you want to display.

How to shut down.

Please press the button for 5 seconds, you will see 5 LEDs turn off in sequence, then the power is off.
