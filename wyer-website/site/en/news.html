Electricity prices have risen, and the issue of renewable energy has been raised again

Electricity prices have risen, and the issue of renewable energy has been raised again. The advantage of generating electricity from natural sources is that it costs nothing, but since natural sources may be affected by weather and seasons, it is important to store electricity when it is available.。

Energy storage equipment includes energy storage components (batteries and other components), power conversion (PCS), and energy management systems (EMS). When the power is in an unstable, the storage device will convert the power to all equipment for a short time, and when the normal power is restored, it can also stabilize the voltage to protect the back-end power equipment, which is a good choice for energy saving and carbon reduction nowadays.
